Lucía graduated from RADA on the BA in Technical Theatre and Stage Management specialising in Lighting Design. Born in Spain, she moved to London to pursue a degree in Natural Sciences. While studying her BSc at UCL, she fell in love with theatre and after completing her degree she decided to change specialism. Lucía enjoys working in theatre and dance, especially with new, innovative collaborations, and is the joint winner of the 2019 ALD Michael Northen Award for Lighting Design.
